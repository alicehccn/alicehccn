About Alice

Alice's painstakingly-composed, deeply narrative images quickly earned the attention and respect of her photographer peers and cutting-edge publications like the popular Capitol Hill Seattle Blog. When not behind a lens, Alice is pursuing the craft of web development, marching for the creative potential ready to be unlocked by her growing mastery of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Ruby.

Alice lives in Seattle with her husband and her beagle Sophie.
